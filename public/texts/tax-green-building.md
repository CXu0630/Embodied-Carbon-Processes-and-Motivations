# Green Building Tax Incentives
### 179D Energy-Efficient Commercial Building Deduction[^1][^2]
**Target**: Commercial and residential buildings with 4+ stories
**Beneficiaries**: Building owners and designers(architects, engineers, contractors) if the owner is tax-exempt
**Condition**: Demonstrate reduction in energy use intensity by 25% relative to the baseline
**Amount**: Up to $5.81 per sqf depending on prevailing wage/apprenticeship compliance, can be claimed multiple times if upgrades occur more than 3 years apart
**Note**: This deduction may no longer apply by June 2026 under the Big Beautiful Bill Act, but has not yet been enacted as of 2025

### 25D Residential Clean Energy Credit[^3][^4]
**Target**: Single-family or multifamily residences used as the taxpayer's home
**Beneficiaries**: Homeowners occupying the residence
**Condition**: Install new qualifying clean-energy equipment (solar PV, solar water heating, geothermal heat pump, small fuel cells)
**Amount**: 30% of installation cost (including equipment, labor, permitting, wiring, interconnection, etc.)
**Note**: rental-only properties are excluded, only the portion of the building that the owner occupies is eligible
